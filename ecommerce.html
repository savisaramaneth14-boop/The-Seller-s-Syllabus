E-Commerce

In 2026 E-commerce is, about people buying and selling things on the internet. This can be goods or services. It also involves moving money or information around on the internet. Over time E-commerce has changed a lot. It used to be simple websites where people could buy things. Now E-commerce is a system that includes social media, special computer programs called AI agents and things that combine the physical and digital worlds, which people call physical-digital or phygital experiences and these phygital experiences are a part of E-commerce.

Usage of E-Commerce

E-commerce is used in ways across various sectors to drive efficiency

  • Retail and Social Commerce: Brands use platforms like TikTok or Instagram to sell products through short videos and live streaming. This way of shopping allows users to buy things away without leaving the app.
  • B2B Procurement: Businesses use e-commerce to buy things in quantities. They use something called Reverse Auctions to find materials and parts from suppliers all around the world. This helps costs compared to using the phone or paper to place orders.
  • Subscription Services: Companies use e-commerce to create ways for people to pay for things regularly. This is common for software media like Netflix and physical goods like meal kits or grooming products that are sent to people on a schedule.
  • Customer Personalization using Artificial Intelligence: E-commerce systems use Artificial Intelligence to look at what people have been browsing and try to figure out what they want before they even search for it. This is used to make the shopping experience very personal for each user so every person sees a version of the store.
  • Service and Digital Goods: E-commerce is not just for things. It is the way we buy digital products like e-books, online courses and services like booking a consultant or a flight.
  • Supply Chain Integration: E-commerce is used to connect the moment someone places an order to the warehouse where the order is prepared. When someone places an order the system automatically updates what is in stock sends the order to the place where it can be fulfilled and tells the shipping company to get ready to send the order. E-commerce systems, like these make it easier for companies to manage their e-commerce and get things to people quickly.
